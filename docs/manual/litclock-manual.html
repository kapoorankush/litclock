Getting started

Your clock asks one thing of you: your home’s Wi‑Fi. After that, it runs on its own.

  1. Plug in the power. Give it a minute to wake up.
  2. Scan the QR code on the screen with your phone’s camera, then wait about 30 seconds. Your phone joins the clock and a setup page opens on its own.
  3. Choose your home Wi‑Fi on that page, type its password, and tap Save.
  4. That’s it. The clock works out your location and the right time on its own, then begins showing quotes.

Where to keep it: LitClock is made to be read up close, the way you’d read a book — not glanced at from across the room. Set it somewhere you’d pause to read: a shelf, a desk, a bedside.

If the setup page doesn’t open on its own, connect your phone to the clock’s own Wi‑Fi (its name and password are on the screen), and it will appear. More fixes are on the last page.

Everyday use

Your phone is the remote

See the small square QR code in the top-right corner of the clock’s screen? Point your phone’s camera at it — a link pops up to tap, and a page opens where you can:

  • set your location — the time and weather follow it
  • switch between Fahrenheit and Celsius
  • show or hide the weather on the screen
  • show or hide quotes with mature content
  • restart the clock, or switch it off

A tip: bookmark this page or add it to your phone’s Home Screen, so it’s one tap away. Either way, you can always open it again by scanning the same QR code on the clock.

Just in case

If something’s not right

The time looks wrong.

Scan the QR code in the top-right corner with your phone and set your location. The clock takes its time from there.

There’s no weather.

Scan the top-right QR code, switch the weather on, and check that your location is set.

It won’t connect to the Wi‑Fi.

Unplug the power, plug it back in, and try the setup steps again. Double-check the password.

I’m moving it to a new home.

Best to reset it before you go: scan the QR code, then choose System → Reset Wi‑Fi. Set it up like new at the new place. If you’ve already moved, just plug in the power and wait about 20 minutes — it returns to setup mode on its own.

Everything else is on the settings page — the QR code in the top-right corner.
Enjoy your minutes.
